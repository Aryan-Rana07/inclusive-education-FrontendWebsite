What is inclusive education? and Why is it necessary?

What is Inclusive Education?

When children or people form diverse backgrounds, culture, needs, come together in a learning system wherein equal opportunities are shared amongst learners despite of these differences; moreover an attitude of respect and acceptance for each other is what inclusive education is about.

Benefits of Inclusive Education

The benefits of inclusive education are that it helps students with greater academic and class participation, increased oportunities to recieve and provide social support, friendship and understanding with people of all kinds of background and thus helping in decreasing racism and discrimination.

why is it necessary?

Inclusive education is necessary to achieve the 4th UN's SDG goal which is quality education, inclusion in education is also helpful in yielding better human beings and not only people who are good at their own field.

Inclusive education includes all types of students from all kind of backgrounds such as people form urban background, rural background and even differently abled people all in one classroom.
