About This Website

This website made to help others by making them aware about the things provided by the government for the help of the children to get better and easier access to education which will eventually lead to inclusive education. This website consists of a form which is called the student form, which is to be filled by the student honestly and carefully to check if their school is equipped to provide inclusion in education for disabled people. The information filled in the form is sent to the email of this website, to be collected and can be used to see what exact number of schools are disability friendly. In the "find opportunities" page you can find yourself or your friends and family the links and information for the educational opportunities provided by different organisations and indian government in the field of education.These organisations may provide resourse, scolorships os any other kind of help to the needy.
